Accessibility

Last updated 2026. Working draft — please review before publication.

Our commitment

Starr Fintech Global is committed to making this website usable for as many people as possible, and we aim to meet the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines as a practical baseline.

What we do

We use semantic HTML, meaningful contrast, keyboard-navigable links and controls, and descriptive labels. Decorative animation is hidden from assistive technology, and all motion respects your prefers-reduced-motion setting — if you have reduced motion enabled in your system, animated backgrounds and transitions are disabled automatically.

Known limitations

This is an evolving site. Some interactive visualizations are presented as decorative and are not intended to convey essential information; equivalent text is provided wherever content matters.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please tell us at msx@sfg.xyz so we can address it.
